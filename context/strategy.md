## Strategic Priorities

1. **Land more small website clients** — primary revenue engine. Target: 3-4 websites/month at higher pricing ($1,800-$2,500). See `context/money-model.md` for pricing model.
2. **Build monthly recurring revenue (MRR)** — every client on hosting ($59-79/mo) + upsells (CRM, SEO, ads). MRR compounds and creates financial stability for the corporate exit.
3. **YouTube content flywheel** — currently posting ~fortnightly, goal is weekly. Build things → document → publish → attract clients. Long-form YouTube → condensed to short-form for IG/TikTok/LinkedIn.
4. **Professionalise operations** — PI insurance, proper service agreements, domain management process, scalable hosting setup. Stop running like a side hustle, start running like an agency.
5. **Prepare for corporate exit** — target: leave 9-5 once MRR + pipeline gives confidence. Then go to 2-3 YouTube posts/week and daily content.
6. **Good reputation and positive impact** — quality over quantity in early client work. Every happy client is a referral source.
