# Referrals & Testimonials

Send to happy clients after they've been with you for 4+ weeks and have seen value. Don't ask too early.

---

## Standard Referral Ask

**Subject:** Quick favour?

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Glad to hear [Business Name]'s website is working well for you — that's awesome.

Quick one — if you know anyone else who could use a hand with their website or getting found online, I'd really appreciate the referral. Most of my best clients have come through word of mouth.

No pressure at all — just thought I'd ask. And if anyone does reach out, I'll make sure they get the same level of service.

Cheers,
Aaron

---

## After Positive Feedback (strike while the iron's hot)

**Subject:** Thanks — and a small ask

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Really appreciate the kind words — means a lot, especially when you're building something from scratch.

Two small asks, if you don't mind:

1. **Google review** — if you've got 2 minutes, a quick review on Google would be massive for me: [Google review link]
2. **Referrals** — if you know any other businesses that could use a website or want to get more leads online, I'd love an intro

Totally optional, but either one would genuinely help. Thanks again.

Cheers,
Aaron

---

## Testimonial Request (for website / social proof)

**Subject:** Would you mind a quick quote?

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I'm putting together some testimonials for my website and was wondering if you'd be happy for me to use a quick quote from you?

Totally fine if not — but if you are, it can be as simple as a line or two about your experience. Something like:

> "Aaron built our website in under a week and we've already had [X] enquiries through it. Really easy to work with."

You can write your own or I can draft something based on our chats and you just approve it — whatever's easier for you.

Cheers,
Aaron

---

## Video Testimonial Ask (high value — use selectively)

**Subject:** Quick video?

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

This is a bigger ask — would you be open to recording a quick 30-second video testimonial? Just on your phone, nothing fancy.

Something like:
- Who you are and what your business does
- What I helped with
- How it's going

Totally understand if that's not your thing — a written quote is just as valuable. But video testimonials carry a lot of weight, so thought I'd ask.

Cheers,
Aaron

---

## Notes for Aaron

- **Timing:** After a positive comment, after a milestone (e.g. "we got 5 leads this week"), or after 4-6 weeks of good service
- **Never:** Ask when there's an outstanding issue or they've just paid a big invoice
- **Google review link:** Get your direct review link from Google Business Profile → Share review form
- **Track referrals:** Note in CRM who referred whom — consider a referral incentive later (e.g. free month of hosting)
- **Make it easy:** "Just forward this email" or "give them my number" — don't make them do work
- **Written testimonials:** Offering to draft it for them gets a much higher response rate. Most people want to help but don't know what to write.
- **Video testimonials:** Only ask your best/most engaged clients. Use on YouTube, website, and social media.
