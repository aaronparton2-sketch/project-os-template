# Upsell Emails

For offering additional services to existing clients. Timing matters — upsell after they've seen value from the current service.

---

## CRM Upsell (after website live 2+ weeks)

**Subject:** Quick idea to help you track your leads better

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Your website's been live for a couple of weeks now and I've noticed you're getting [X enquiries / some good traffic]. Nice one.

Quick thought — right now your leads come through as emails, which works. But I can set up a simple CRM (a Google Sheet that auto-populates) so every lead lands in one place with their name, phone, email, and message. Makes it way easier to follow up and not let anyone slip through the cracks.

It's a quick setup — $[price] one-off, then $[price]/mo to keep it running. Want me to show you how it works?

Cheers,
Aaron

---

## Notes for Aaron

- **Timing is everything.** Don't upsell on Day 1. Wait until they've seen value.
- **Lead with their problem**, not your service
- **Use data if you have it** — "you've had 12 enquiries this month" is more compelling than "you could get more leads"
- **One upsell per email.** Don't bundle CRM + SEO + Ads into one email.
- **If they say no:** Respect it. Try again in 2-3 months when circumstances may have changed.
