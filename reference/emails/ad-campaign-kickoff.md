# Ad Campaign Kickoff Email

Send after client agrees to paid ad management. Sets expectations and gathers what you need.

---

## Google Ads + Meta Ads Kickoff

**Subject:** Getting your ads started — what I need from you

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Great news — let's get your ads up and running. Here's what I need from you and what to expect.

**What I need from you:**

1. **Photos/videos of your work** — real photos always outperform stock images. Even phone photos are fine. The more the better — team shots, equipment, finished jobs, happy customers (with permission).

2. **Credit card for ad spend** — I'll add this to the ad accounts during setup. The ad platforms (Google/Facebook) charge you directly — the ad spend goes straight to them, not to me.

3. **Access** — I've attached a step-by-step guide that walks you through giving me access to your Facebook Page, Instagram, and Google Business Profile. It should take about 10 minutes. (See `reference/emails/client-access-guide.md`)

4. **Quick answers:**
   - What's your main service area? (towns/suburbs/radius)
   - What's one job worth to you on average? (helps me calculate target cost per lead)
   - Any services you want to push more than others?

**What to expect:**

- **Week 1:** I'll set up the campaigns, install tracking on your website, and get ads live
- **First 3-5 days:** The ad platforms are "learning" — results may be patchy. This is normal.
- **Week 2+:** Results stabilise. I'll optimise based on what's working.
- **Ongoing:** You'll get a weekly text/WhatsApp update with spend, leads, and cost per lead

**When leads come in:**
- You'll get an email notification for every new lead
- **Call them back within 1 hour if possible** — speed is the #1 factor in converting ad leads to customers
- If you can't call, even a quick text ("Thanks for your enquiry, I'll call you shortly") helps

**Costs:**
- Ad setup fee: $[price] (one-off)
- Monthly management: $[price]/mo
- Ad spend: $[X]/week (paid directly to Google/Facebook)

Any questions, just let me know. I'll aim to have everything live within [X days].

Cheers,
Aaron
Mycelium AI

## Notes for Aaron

- Send this **after** payment for setup fee
- Adjust sections based on whether it's Google only, Meta only, or both
- The "speed to lead" reminder is critical — embed it early so they're ready when leads arrive
- Follow up if you don't get photos within 2-3 days — you can start with whatever's on their Facebook/website
- Link to `paid-ads` skill for discovery questions if not already completed
- **Always attach `client-access-guide.md`** (or paste relevant sections) — saves you 30+ mins of back-and-forth on calls/texts
