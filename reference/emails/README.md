# Email Templates

Reusable email templates for common business interactions. Copy, personalise, and send.

**Tone:** Friendly, casual-professional. "Cheers" not "Regards". Clear, not corporate.

**Rules:**
- Always personalise — name, business, specific details
- Never send a template without reading it through first
- Keep subject lines short and specific

---

## Templates

| File | When to Use |
|------|-------------|
| `onboarding.md` | After client payment clears — welcome email with setup details |
| `benchmark-pricing.md` | Reaching out to agencies for competitor pricing research |
| `cold-outreach.md` | Cold email to potential clients (businesses without a good web presence) |
| `follow-up.md` | Follow-up after discovery call, after quote, or after silence |
| `upsell.md` | Offering additional services to existing clients (CRM, SEO, ads) |
| `payment-chaser.md` | Failed payment or overdue invoice — friendly then firm |
| `offboarding.md` | Client leaving — professional exit, handover details, door open |
| `referrals-testimonials.md` | Asking for referrals, Google reviews, written/video testimonials |
| `ad-campaign-kickoff.md` | Onboarding email when starting paid ads management for a client |
| `client-access-guide.md` | Attach to ad kickoff — step-by-step guide for clients to grant Facebook, Instagram, Google access |
