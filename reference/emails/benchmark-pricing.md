# Benchmark Pricing Email

Send to competitor agencies when researching market pricing. Pose as a potential customer.

---

## Initial Enquiry

**Subject:** Quick question about your web design services

**Body:**

Hi [Agency Name] team,

I'm looking at getting a website built for my small business and wanted to get a rough idea of your pricing before booking a call.

I need a simple landing page / small business website — about 3-5 pages, contact form, mobile-friendly. Nothing too complex.

Could you give me a ballpark on:
- What a build like that would cost?
- Whether there's a monthly hosting/maintenance fee?
- Rough turnaround time?

Happy to jump on a quick call if that's easier, but even a rough range by email would be helpful.

Thanks,
[Name]

---

## Notes for Aaron

- Use a personal email (not mycelium.ai) for these
- Keep records of responses in `context/outstanding-actions.md` or a spreadsheet
- You're benchmarking — not trying to waste their time. Keep it brief.
- Target a mix: freelancers, small agencies, and established agencies in Perth/your market
- Record: agency name, build price range, monthly fees, turnaround, what's included
