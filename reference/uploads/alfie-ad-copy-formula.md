# Alfie's Ad Copy Formula

**Source:** Alfie Excalidraw — Level 2 (Paid Ads 101)
**Context:** Used to scale AI agency to $50k/mo. $1,642 spent → 230 leads.

---

## The Formula

```
Industry Lingo  →  Pain Point  →  Body  →  Offer  →  CTA (Free Demo)
```

### Breakdown

| Element | What it does | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Industry Lingo** | Stops the scroll — they recognise their world | "This Solar Business..." / "Do you have over 10,000 old leads?" |
| **Pain Point** | Names the problem they're living with | "Stopped relying on buying new expensive leads, that killed their margin..." |
| **Body** | Shows the solution + proof (numbers) | "They got an AI to reach out to leads already paid for. 48% responded. 11% booked." |
| **Offer** | What you'll do for them + risk reversal | "We build the AI and tailored it to their business 100% for FREE. ZERO upfront costs." |
| **CTA** | Single clear action | "Try our Demo for Free — click Learn More below" |

## Key Patterns

1. **Lead with a specific result** — not "we do AI", but "41 meetings in 3 days"
2. **Name the industry** — "solar business", not "businesses"
3. **Use numbers everywhere** — 48%, 11%, 500+, $792k, 30 days
4. **Risk reversal is the closer** — "100% for FREE", "ZERO upfront costs", "only paid on closed deals"
5. **Short paragraphs** — 1-2 lines max, lots of white space, reads like a text message
6. **Ellipses build suspense** — "The result?..." keeps them reading
7. **CTA is always "Learn More"** — not "Sign Up" or "Buy Now" (lower commitment)
8. **Always "Free Demo"** — not "free consultation" or "free quote"

## How Aaron Could Adapt This

Replace solar/AI agent context with website/automation context:

**Industry Lingo:** "This [plumber/electrician/tradie] in [Perth/Toowoomba]..."
**Pain Point:** "...was losing leads because they had no website / their website looked like it was built in 2005"
**Body:** "We built them a professional website + automated their lead capture. [X] enquiries in the first [timeframe]."
**Offer:** "We'll build a demo version for FREE — if you don't like it, no charge."
**CTA:** "Click Learn More to see a demo"
