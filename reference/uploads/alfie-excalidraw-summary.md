## Level 1: Get to $10k/mo (Service Business)

### Origin Story
- Two paths: 5 years at uni learning software dev OR go all in on AI
- Chose AI → met a random guy in Melbourne who was starting an AI company
- Got home worried about paying rent, $50 in the bank
- 15 appointments, 0 closed, $0 revenue, 0% close rate for 4 months (12-hour days)
- "Most growth in my life. F*ck the money (for now)"

### The Breakthrough: Free Website Strategy
1. **Google Maps** → find businesses with no website
2. **Call them** → offer a free website (use local phone number)
3. **Discovery call** → ask about what website they want
4. **Build it** → use a template from website builder (Go High Level), do a good job
5. **Show it on a call** → give it free + get a video testimonial
6. **Upsell, upsell, upsell** — keep offering until they say no:

### The Upsell Ladder
| Service | Setup | Monthly | Pitch |
|---------|-------|---------|-------|
| Free website | Free (decoy offer) | — | "Let me build you a free website" |
| CRM / lead storage | $100 setup | $97/mo | "Now you need a place to store all the leads" |
| Website widget (chatbot) | $200 setup | $97/mo | "Capture even more traffic — I already created a demo" |
| Voice agent (after-hours) | $300 setup | $297/mo | "People will try and call you outside of opening hours" |
| Google Review agent | $250 setup | $197/mo | "We should get more Google reviews we can display on the website" |
| Facebook Ads | $250 setup | $397/mo | "We can run some extra traffic to your new website" |
| SMS/Email lead reactivation | $0 upfront | $50/appt | Down-sell offer when they say no |

**Key tactic — Proof of Concept offer:** "I'll build the entire thing for you so you can see what it will look like. If you don't like it, full refund." Removes risk, makes it easy to say yes.

**Additional offer:** "If you say yes on this call... first month free."

**Result:** "That's how I made my first $400 in month 4. If I did this from the start, I would have done it in month 1."

### Goal: Become their "internet partner"
- $97/mo + $197/mo + $197/mo = stacking MRR per client
- Keep offering services until they say no

### The Grind Phase
- Minimal money, no leverage, can't hire anyone yet
- Need first 3 clients for: sales experience, service delivery, a little money to reinvest into ads
- Closed first couple clients with cold outreach + cold calling on Google Maps

## Level 2: Scale to $50k/mo (Paid Ads)

### Paid Ads 101

**Campaign 1: Retargeting ($5/day)**
1. Install Facebook Pixel (track visitors)
2. Create audience with website visitors (180 days)
3. Create a manual campaign — lead form ads retargeting
4. Add creative

**Campaign 2: Lead Form Campaign ($15/day)**
- "Only $1,642 spent" → 230 leads
- Best performing copy includes proof-heavy storytelling (solar business example: "AI booked 500+ appointments in 30 days, $792k in sales from dead leads")
- Headlines: "500+ Appointments booked. Try AI Demo" / "Do you have over 10,000 old leads? Try AI Demo"
- Always use "Learn More" CTA
- Add qualifying questions to lead form (team size filter)
- Call leads as soon as they come in (under 1 minute)
- Split test ads and rotate when performance drops

### Scaling Ads
- Spend more money + split test more creative
- "With ads we scaled our AI agency to $50k/mo + worked with 2 billion dollar companies"

### Ad Copy Formula
```
Industry Lingo → Pain Point → Body → Offer → CTA (Free Demo)
```

## Level 3: Build a $1M SAAS

**Important:** Don't start here. It takes money + a lot of time.

### Build Process
- **Step 0:** F*ck Figma — you'll waste time. Test your MVP ASAP.
- **Step 1:** Find initial frontend from v0 marketplace (better than Lovable)
- **Step 2:** Download the code from v0 (as ZIP — important: you get the code)
- **Step 3:** Open folder in Visual Studio Code, unzip
- **Step 4:** Use Factory Droid from Factory.ai (cheaper + better than Cursor)
- **Step 5:** Tell Droid to add: Payments (Stripe), Authentication, Database, AI Calling (VAPI)
- **Step 6:** Instruct Droid in building your software

### 3 Tips to Become a GOD Vibe Coder
1. Always include specific instructions in your prompt (AI gets terrible when code > 1000 lines)
2. For detailed implementations, break them down
3. Get it to plan changes, then ask to implement

### Launch Strategy
- **LAUNCH EARLY.** Best way to improve software is to get it in users' hands.
- Don't offer free trials when you start — figure out if people will pay
- "Don't end up like this guy" — spent 1 year building and got 0 users

### Tech Stack Mentioned
- **Payments:** Stripe (or alternatives)
- **Auth:** Built-in
- **Database:** Supabase-style
- **AI Calling:** VAPI
- **Bonus:** Sign up to Microsoft Founders Hub for $500 in free credits (sign up as software, not agency)

## Level 4: How to Get 10M Views a Month

### Content Strategy (grew to 235k followers in 8 months)

**Core rule:** Post once a day with LOTS of different formats for your niche.

**Best performing formats:**
- Authority in hook
- Split screen
- "No talk" videos

**When you find an outlier (10x views than normal) → DOUBLE DOWN**

### What Makes a Viral Video (2 things)
1. **Average Watchtime + Skip Rate** — low skip rate, high avg watch time, flat curve
2. **Like to Save Ratio** — more saves than likes = valuable

### Content Ideas (for AI agencies)
1. Learn how to create a new agent (voice agent, n8n flow, automate something)
2. In the hook: explain WHO it can help (the industry — plumbers, roofers, dentists)
3. In the hook: show a demo of what it does
4. In the body: show a quick tutorial of how to build it (positions you as an expert)
5. In the CTA: "If you want a full tutorial comment 'X'" (farms engagement + followers)

### Bonus Tips
- **Farm followers:** "Are you following?" as first message, comment "X" for resource
- **Use GHL instead of ManyChat** — saves thousands when you blow up
- **For your own software:** Use your own software in the videos
- **Scale further:** Pay other influencers to promote (what they're doing now)
