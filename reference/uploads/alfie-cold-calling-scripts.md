# Alfie's Cold Calling Scripts

**Source:** Alfie Excalidraw — extracted directly from embedded links/content
**Context:** These are Alfie's top-converting cold call scripts for AI agency sales. Adapted for reference — Aaron should personalise for Mycelium AI's services (websites, automation, not just AI sales reps).

---

## Script 1: The Confused Opener

> **(Remember to sound confused — ESSENTIAL)**
>
> "Yeah, [name] there?"
>
> "Yeah, [name]."
>
> "I was looking into [company name] and I was wondering if you could help me out for a moment?" *(Pause)*
>
> "I'm not even sure if it makes sense for us to talk yet but we follow up with all aged leads that you never converted to increase your new sales, while you only pay if it generates revenue?"
>
> "And they asked me to reach out to you to see if you'd be opposed to boosting your sales using AI completely result based? Would you be opposed to that?"

**Why it works:** Confused tone disarms. "Would you be opposed" is a negative close — hard to say yes to opposing free money.

---

## Script 2: The CEO Direct Reach

> "Is it [firstName]?"
>
> "[firstName], I'm sorry this is [yourName] I'm the CEO of [companyName] and I don't normally make calls about this, but our advisory board told me about your company. We usually help [industry] companies generate more sales through AI and I'm taking a couple people and I just figured to get a feel for the market, to call you directly as well."
>
> "Do you have a sales team for your sales?"
>
> "Do you have a lot of leads that haven't turned into contracts?"
>
> "Do you have an idea why some of these leads aren't closing and what could improve conversion rate?"
>
> "Do you think your sales team is losing out on sales because of bad follow up?"
>
> "One of the things that we've done for another company like yours *(be specific if possible)*, is create a customized AI and add it to our sales process that engages and follows up with our leads on SMS to find interest and book appointments to help drive more revenue. I'd be happy to show you my system for free if you want? How about we set a meeting [time] and let me show you... I'll do it personally?"

**Why it works:** CEO positioning + "advisory board told me" = flattery + authority. Discovery questions before pitching. Free demo offer removes risk.

## Script 3: The Name Drop

> "Hey [firstName]"
>
> "We work with a few other [industry] companies like [competitor/known name]"
>
> "It's [yourName] at [companyName], have you heard our name tossed around?"
>
> "We add AI to the sales team to close more deals..."

**Why it works:** Short. Social proof via name drop. "Have you heard our name" is curiosity-driven — they almost always say no, which opens the door to explain.

---

## Script 4: The Honest Cold Call

> "Hi [firstName], we don't know each other, if I mention this is a cold call would that ruin your [day]?"
>
> "I'm with an AI company that adds AI sales reps to the team to increase conversion rates. I talk to [position] daily to help them improve their sales team by adding AI. But I have a feeling you're about to tell me your sales team is already the best?"
>
> *(When they respond)* "Why are they so good, and how can we make them better?"

**Why it works:** Radical honesty disarms. Self-deprecating humour. The "your team is already the best" line is reverse psychology — they either agree (opening for "how can we make them better") or disagree (opening for the pitch).

## Aaron's Adaptation Notes

These scripts are written for AI sales rep / lead reactivation services. For Mycelium AI's website + automation services, the core frameworks translate:

- **Script 1 (Confused):** Adapt for "free website" offer — "would you be opposed to a free website for your business?"
- **Script 2 (CEO Direct):** Adapt discovery questions for web presence — "do you have a website?", "how do customers find you online?", "do you think you're missing leads because people can't find you?"
- **Script 3 (Name Drop):** Use local Perth businesses as social proof
- **Script 4 (Honest):** Works as-is with service swap — "I build websites and automations for trades businesses"

**Priority:** Cold email first (automation), then personalised cold call scripts for follow-up. Aaron's goal is leads via automation + referrals, with phone calls as a daily 1-hour power session.
