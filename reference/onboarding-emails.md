## Notes for Aaron

- Always personalise — use their name, their domain, their specific setup
- Send within 24 hours of site going live
- If client has both website + CRM, use the combined template (don't send two emails)
- For upsells added later (SEO, ads), send the relevant add-on template as a standalone
- Keep tone casual — "cheers" not "regards", "just message me" not "please don't hesitate to contact"
